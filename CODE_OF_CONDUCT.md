# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want FerricStore to be a respectful, practical engineering community. Contributors are expected to communicate clearly, assume good intent, and focus criticism on technical work rather than people.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep discussions technical and actionable.
- Accept feedback and provide evidence for claims.
- Avoid harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or harassment.
- Discriminatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.
